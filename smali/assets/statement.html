• Safety Tips

  1. This product should not be used by those under the age of 18 or with no full capacity for civil conduct.
  2. Featured by quite large dimension of body, high-speed propeller and powerful flight dynamics, this product is quite dangerous while operating; in case of failure in operating or using this product based on requirement, the user may suffer from any danger or damage.
  3. While using this product, please keep it off hazardous environment, such as airport, railway, expressway, high-rise building and wire.
  4. While using this product, please keep it off high electromagnetism disturbance environment, such as mobile phone base station and high-power radiation equipment.
  5. While using this product, please keep it off all kinds of manned aircraft.
  6. Please do avoid using this product at bad environment, such as rain, thunder, sand storm, fog, snow, wind and low temperature.
  7. While flying this product above 3,000 m altitude, please do use this product carefully, for the environmental factor will decline flight performance.
  8. As this product is non-waterproof, please do avoid landing it on water surface.
  9. If operating this product at low altitude, please always keep the aircraft at safety distance which is above 10 m off human people and animal.
  10. If using this product at populated or densely populated area, please always keep the aircraft within your view sight.
  11. Please do not hover or fly this product over the crowd or make amusement by frightening others.
  12. If the crowd approaches, please operate this product carefully and land it at the site as soon as possible for fear of any accident.
  13. Please do not operate this product at the site where kids are playing.
  14. Please avoid chasing the traffic tools or affecting normal operation of the traffic tools with this product.
  15. If unnecessary, please do avoid switching off the motors while this product is flying in the air.
  16. Please avoid using this product when drinking alcohol, keeping fatigue, taking drug or feeling uncomfortable.
